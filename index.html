Light and Sound Memory Game

Welcome to the light and sound memory game, where you will be putting your memorization skills to the test!

You will be given a random pattern of eight clues that you will progressively remember and immitate. However,
be careful, the game speeds up every time you guess the pattern correctly!

First, select the amount of buttons you would like to play with. Next, press Start when you are ready to begin.
Didn't like your choice? Simply press Restart to change the amount of buttons you would like to play with.
